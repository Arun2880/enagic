Welcome To KANGEN WATER.

Kangen means "return to origin" in Japanese. Kangen Water is alkaline water produced by Enagic's alkaline ionizer and water filtration machines. For more than 40 years, Kangen Water has been used in Japan to help restore the body to its original, alkaline state. These machines can transform your ordinary tap water into healthy, fresh tasting alkaline drinking water. Kangen Water is superior to tap and RO water.

How is Enagic Kangen water Made?

Filtration

Tap water first passes through a multi-stage filtration system that removes impurities, chlorine, and other contaminants.

Electrolysis

The purified water then enters an electrolysis chamber, which contains platinum-coated titanium electrode plates. An electric current is applied, causing the water molecules to split into acidic and alkaline components.

Separation

The acidic water is discharged, while the alkaline water, now rich in hydrogen and minerals, is retained. This is the Kangen Water, which is then ready for various uses.
