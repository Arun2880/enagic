What is Enagic Kangen water?

Enagic Kangen water is a type of alkaline water produced by Kangen Water Enagic’s innovative water ionization technology. The term "Kangen" means "return to origin" in Japanese, reflecting the water's clean and pure state. It is known for its high pH level and antioxidant properties, making it a popular choice for those seeking a refreshing and versatile hydration solution.
